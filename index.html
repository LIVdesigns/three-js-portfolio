About Me

I am an undergraduate at San Jose State University, pursuing a BFA in Digital Media Art with aspirations of becoming a web developer and graphic designer. I am a determined and organized individual, with strong communication skills that make me an effective collaborator within a team. My passion lies in helping others bring their creative visions to life, ensuring their satisfaction with the final result. While my primary focus is digital media, I also enjoy working with traditional art forms, particularly charcoal and paper, which allows me to explore creativity across various mediums.
